About the page developer

Victor was born in Manitoba, Canada and has been involved in the technology field since a young age. In elementary school he took his first computer classes, including web development in grade 6. In high school he continued his learning by taking his first steps in computer animation, AutoCAD, and music production. As an adult he worked in the field of oil and gas as a machinist, before transitioning into a developer during the 2020 pandemic. Initially focusing on computer game development he now seeks to broaden his understanding of the field by learning web, mobile, and desktop development.


In his free time he likes to play video games, create 3D illustrations using Blender, and occasionally makes electronic music using computer DAWs and hardware synthesizers.


He currently lives in Drumheller with his wife, 2 cats, and dog.
